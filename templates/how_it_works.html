How It Works

This is an Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN) exam preparation application. Here you can practice quizzes or memorise terms with our flash cards.

Our website is optimised for mobile. Infact, the website incoporates a mobile first philosophy so you can easily have access to ican.mumu from your phone. This means you can continue with your preparations in traffic, during boring meetings and all the oddest places apart from inside a pool or in the shower.

If you can answer every question on the site correctly and correctly answer every flash card question then you can be sure of ICAN success.
For best results, make it a point of duty to visit every other day.

Keep track of your "Hotness"

There is a "hotness bar" which shows after you answer each quiz question. It shows your performance on previous attempts at each question. A red shows a wrong answer and a green shows a correct answer.

W C C C correct

Be sure to always make sure you hotness bar looks like that which you see above and is always green. If you hotness bar for a particular keeps showing red the it is an indicator of a need to study that topic more.

P.S You need to be logged in to be able to see a hotness bar.
