How It Works

So What Exactly Is This Website About?

This is a revision question bank for those preparing for The Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN) forthcoming professional examinations.

If you require something to help you guage your preparedness or you need something extra to add to your preparation efforts, then this is a valuable resource for you.

There are multiple choice questions and then open ended questions. For each type, you can choose for the website engine to throw you questions from a whole level, or from a whole paper, or you can get specific and choose to get questions from a particular topic.

They Are ICAN Past Questions Abi?

....em, NO. Our website is not an ICAN past question dump like you find a lot these days. A lot of our questions are modelled on ICAN past questions though but if ICAN past questions are enough to pass, there should be almost no failures in ICAN exams. A lot of people still fail after using just past questions to practice and that's why we also model our question based on ICAN recommended books.

What Are Open Ended Questions?

We show you a simple question. You answer it aloud or in your mind (just try to answer it), click the 'View Answer' button and the answer is shown to you so you can assess yourself. Click here to view the available open ended quizzes

What About Multiple Choice Questions?

Simple, we show you a question with a bunch of options to choose from. When you submit, you find out if you were right or wrong. We even put links to resources on the web concerning the question you just answered. Click here to view the available multiple choice quizzes.

Keep track of your "Hotness"

There is a "hotness bar" which shows after you answer each question in the multiple choice section. It shows your performance on previous attempts at each question. A red shows a wrong answer and a green shows a correct answer.

W C C C correct

The greener the bar, the better. If your hotness bar for a particular question keeps showing red, it is an indicator of a need to study that topic more.

P.S You need to be logged in to be able to see a hotness bar.
