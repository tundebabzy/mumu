How It Works

mumu.com is a quiz application that helps you prepare for the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN) examinations free of charge.

The quiz presents you with questions based on the category you select so you can test yourself on a topic or you can test yourself on a whole paper or even better, you can test yourself on a whole level.

Our website is optimised for mobile. Infact, the website incoporates a mobile first philosophy so you can easily have access to mumu.com from your phone. This means you can continue with your preparations in traffic, during boring meetings and all the oddest places.

Memorize with Flash cards

Use our flash cards to help you memorize terms, advantages and disadvantages, types and all those things you know you will definitely need to write out in the exam.
