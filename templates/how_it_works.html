How It Works

If you can answer every question on the site correctly, you are probably going to have a pass.
For best results, make it a point of duty to visit every other day.

Revise with open ended questions

There are hundreds of open ended questions in our database grouped by topics. The questions will be selected randomly and is a good way to check your readiness for open ended and fill-in-the-gap questions.

Revise with multiple choice questions

We also have many multiple choice questions grouped by level, paper and topic. The questions will also be selected randomly and is a good way to check your readiness for the multiple choice questions.

Start

Keep track of your "Hotness"

There is a "hotness bar" which shows after you answer each question in the multiple choice section. It shows your performance on previous attempts at each question. A red shows a wrong answer and a green shows a correct answer.

W C C C correct

The greener the bar, the better. If your hotness bar for a particular question keeps showing red, it is an indicator of a need to study that topic more.

P.S You need to be logged in to be able to see a hotness bar.
